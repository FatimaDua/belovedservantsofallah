Islam As Truly It is - Written by me

Part Five

The relationship between women and men and why our societies have become so backward, degenerate, and miserable.

Allah has created both men and women in order to complete each other and not compete with each other.

Allah says in Surah Ar-Rum verse 21 :

وَجَعَلَ بَيْنَكُم مَّوَدَّةً وَرَحْمَةً

"And He placed between you affection and mercy"

Allah orders women and men to stand together and respect one another.

Before any feminism movements or any of this nonesense we keep hearing about nowadays.

Women in Islam were and still free and dignified and deeply appreciated.

Before Islam, women used to get buried just because they were women. For, it was considered a shame back then to be a woman. Then, Islam came and freed women from all forms of cruelty and injustice.

Allah says in the Quran addressing men, in Surah An-Nisa verse 19 :

وعاشروهن بالمعروف "And be good to women"

Which means, treat your women well, respect them, love them and be kind to them.

Our beloved prophet Muhammad peace be upon him says about women :

النساء شقائق الرجال

"Women are men's sisters"

And he says :

واستوصوا بالنساء خيرا

"Treat women kindly"

And he says :

لا تكرهوا البنات انهن المؤنسات الغاليات

"Do not hate girls, they are the precious consolers"

Our beloved prophet Muhammad peace be upon him always cared for women as he cared for all human beings and treated everyone with kindness and justice.

Allah says about our beloved prophet Muhammad peace be upon him, in Surah Al-Anbya verse 107 : وما أرسلناك إلا رحمة للعالمين

"And We have not sent you, [O Muhammad], except as a mercy to the worlds."

Our beloved prophet Muhammad peace be upon him came to save all human beings from the darkness of ignorance and introduced them to the light of the true guidance which is Islam.

Our beloved prophet Muhammad peace be upon him, being a prophet and a leader of an Ummah ( a nation ), he also helped his wives in the kitchen and the house chores.

His wife Aicha peace be upon her says :

كان الرسول صلى الله عليه وسلم شديد العناية بالنساء، وأدل دليل على ذلك ما ورد في وصيته بهن وبالعناية بحقوقهن والإحسان إليهن في حجة الوداع؛ حين قال: فاتقوا الله في النساء فإنكم أخذتموهن بأمان الله.

"The prophet Muhammad peace be upon him cared a lot about women, and the best proof was his commandments on women during his final sermon in the farewell Pilrimage. Those commandments include taking good care of women and taking care of their rights. He said , addressing men, ' Fear Allah, be conscious of Allah with regards to women and the rights of women. For, you have taken them with the protection of Allah." Aicha, may Allah be pleased with her, also says :

"كان رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم يخصف نعله، ويخيط ثوبه، ويعمل في بيته كما يعمل أحدكم في بيته”

"The prophet peace be upon him, used to repair his own shoes, sew his own clothes, and he also did the house chores as you people do your house chores."

"فتذكر ان محمدا صعد إلى السماء السابعة ووصل الى سدرة المنتهى وإقترب حيث لم يقترب مخلوقا من قبل، ولما عاد إلى الأرض كان في خدمة أهله. كان يحلب شاته، ويخصف نعله، ويأتي العبد يطلب ان يتوسط له عند سيده فيفعل، وتأتي الجارية الصغيرة تجره من يده ليشفع لها عند اهلها فيمضي معها. صعد الى السماء السابعة وبقي يأكل في صحن واحد مع المساكين، ويركب بغله، ويأمر جيشه أن لا يقطعوا شجرا ولا يقتلوا طفلا ولا امرأة، وأن يتركوا الرهبان في اديرتهم هم وما يعبدون. كان كبيرا قبل ان يصعد ، وظل كبيرا بعد ان نزل. كبيرا دون تكبر، عظيما دون تعاظم."

"And remember, that Muhammad ascended to the seventh sky and he reached the Lote Tree of the Utmost boundary. He were at a place no creature had ever been to before. And when he went back home, on earth, he served his people. He milked his own ewe and he repaired his own shoes. In addition to that, when a servant came to the prophet Muhammad peace be upon him and asked him to come in between him (the servant) and his master, the prophet Muhammad would do that. And When a young maid came to him and pulled the prophet Muhammad's hand in order to take him with her to ask forgiveness from her family on her behalf, he would go with her and ask for forgiveness on her behalf.

He ascended to the seventh sky and he still ate from one dish with the poor and the needy, rode his mule, and ordered his army not to cut trees and not to kill babies and women, and to leave the monks alone in their temples with whatever idols they worshipped. Our beloved prophet Muhammad peace be upon him was great before he had ascended to the seventh sky, and he remained great when he went back down. He was great without arrogance.
