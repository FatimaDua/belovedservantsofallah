Islam As Truly It is - Written by me

Part One

. I'm not an expert or a Sheikh, with all due respect to them. But, I am Muslim and I want to share my own humble thoughts about Islam with you ...

I'll start with discussing Salah الصلاة or prayer ...

We all know that Salah is one of the five pillars of Islam and we also know how important it is.



One of the reasons why some people feel nothing when they perform a certain prayer is that they don't pray with their heart. How do you want to feel relieved after praying when you perform your Salah as if it's a burden on you instead of performing it as if it's your only medicine ? It is your only medicine. A medicine to your heart, to free it from all the shackles of grief and sorrow and exhaustion and selfishness and fear and worry.

Islam means total submission to God. How are you going to submit if you are a slave to your ego ?

Allah doesn't want your ego, he wants your heart. Your bare soul. Because of your ego and your doubt and your non-stop negative thoughts, you don't feel relieved after praying.

When you truly and wholeheartedly believe and build a firm belief within. Your whole life will change for the better.

Yes, when praying you must be clean, clean clothes and clean body, and also, a clean heart.

Dear readers, we need to clean our hearts first in order to feel the Salah deeply and feel that priceless and relieving connection with Allah.

A clean heart requires patience of course. This is why you must battle your own evil. Battle hatred, arrogance, laziness, envy, grudges, anger ... these weaken the heart. As our beloved prophet Muhammad peace be upon him says " the greatest Jihad is to battle your own soul, to fight the evil within yourself."

Dear, your heart must be divine. A heart that carries love. A heart that always fights evil. A heart that is so deeply attached to Allah is a clean heart. For, the stronger is your relationship with Allah, the bigger and the kinder your heart becomes.

Allah says in surah a'raad ( Thunder ) , verse number 28 :

الذين آمنوا وتطمئن قلوبهم بذكر الله ألا بذكر الله تطمئن القلوب

"Those who have believed and whose hearts find comfort in the remembrance of Allah. Verily, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find peace."

Allah says in Surah Al ma'arij , verses 19-23 :

إِنَّ الْإِنْسَانَ خُلِقَ هَلُوعًا (19) إِذَا مَسَّهُ الشَّرُّ جَزُوعًا (20) وَإِذَا مَسَّهُ الْخَيْرُ مَنُوعًا (21) إِلَّا الْمُصَلِّينَ (22) الَّذِينَ هُمْ عَلَى صَلَاتِهِمْ دَائِمُونَ (23)

"Verily, mankind was created anxious

He is fretful when misfortune touches him, but tight-fisted when good fortune comes his way. Except those who pray and are constant in their prayers"

Allah says in Surah Al Anfal , verse number 2 :

إِنَّمَا الْمُؤْمِنُونَ الَّذِينَ إِذَا ذُكِرَ اللَّهُ وَجِلَتْ قُلُوبُهُمْ وَإِذَا تُلِيَتْ عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتُهُ زَادَتْهُمْ إِيمَانًا وَعَلَىٰ رَبِّهِمْ يَتَوَكَّلُونَ (2)

"The true believers are those who, when Allah's name is mentioned, their hearts quake, and when His verses are recited to them their faith grows, and who put their trust in their Lord"

Allah says in Surah Al Maidah , verse 83 :

وَإِذَا سَمِعُوا مَا أُنزِلَ إِلَى الرَّسُولِ تَرَىٰ أَعْيُنَهُمْ تَفِيضُ مِنَ الدَّمْعِ مِمَّا عَرَفُوا مِنَ الْحَقِّ ۖ يَقُولُونَ رَبَّنَا آمَنَّا فَاكْتُبْنَا مَعَ الشَّاهِدِينَ (83)

"And when they listen to what has been sent down to the Messenger ( Muhammad peace be upon him ), you see their eyes overflowing with tears, because of the truth they have recognized. They say: “Our Lord! We believe; so write us down among the witnesses.” "

So, the first step to perform your Salah well is to clean your heart, smother your ego, and give your heart to Allah. Then, he will give you an eternal inner tranquillity you never dreamed of before. This happens, when your belief is too firm to be shaken and when your heart is too attached to Allah to be saddened or sick. A heart that knows Allah, knows relief.
