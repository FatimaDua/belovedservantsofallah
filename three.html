Islam As Truly It is - Written by me

Part Three

From my own experience, the truth exposes itself to me naturally, when I truly listen to my inner voice. The voice of nature itself calling me constantly. The voice of truth. Years of research mean too little compared to that precious essence inside of every one of us. For, there are things that don't need logical analysis or a specific, scientific and a final answer, they only need your heart's sincere witness. They need your soul's light. Only then, the truth highlights itself to you. Being an analytic and a deep obersever teaches you a lot of things. Stay awake! There are things that are meant to be wholeheartedly believed and not meant to be proved or given a specific or a scientific explanation. Everything already proves itself though, but sometimes, we are just too blind to see. Simply, because we have too little knowledge of things.

Allah says in Surah Al-Isra , verse 85 :

وَمَا أُوتِيتُم مِّنَ الْعِلْمِ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا.

"Indeed, you ( people ) have been given only little knowledge"

However, Allah has blessed us with something that means everything. It is the pure and the divine essence that always shows us the way to the absolute truth : Allah.

He clearly says in Surah Al-A'raf , verse 198 :

وَإِن تَدْعُوهُمْ إِلَى الْهُدَىٰ لَا يَسْمَعُوا ۖ وَتَرَاهُمْ يَنظُرُونَ إِلَيْكَ وَهُمْ لَا يُبْصِرُونَ.

"And if you invite them to guidance, they do not hear; and you see them looking at you while they do not see ( observe the truth )"

Allah has already given us everything we need. We lack nothing, but we need to see through the eyes of our souls. To see the truth within us and around us. As Allah says in different and many verses in the Quran :

كَذَٰلِكَ يُبَيِّنُ اللَّهُ لَكُمُ الْآيَاتِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَعْقِلُونَ

"Thus Allah makes his injunctions clear to you that you may understand"

Surah Al-Baqara, verse 242.

And he says in Surah Adh-Dhaariyat, verse 21 :

وَفِي أَنفُسِكُمْ ۚ أَفَلَا تُبْصِرُونَ

"And it's ( the truth ) within yourselves, don't you see ? "

And he says in Surah Ar-rum , verse 8 :

أَوَلَمْ يَتَفَكَّرُوا فِي أَنفُسِهِم ۗ مَّا خَلَقَ اللَّهُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا إِلَّا بِالْحَقِّ وَأَجَلٍ مُّسَمًّى ۗ وَإِنَّ كَثِيرًا مِّنَ النَّاسِ بِلِقَاءِ رَبِّهِمْ لَكَافِرُونَ (8)

"Do they not contemplate within themselves? Allah has not created the heavens and the earth and what is between them except in truth and for a specified term. And indeed, many of the people, in [the matter of] the meeting with their Lord, are disbelievers." And he says in Surah Al-an'aam, verse 50 :

قُلْ هَلْ يَسْتَوِي الْأَعْمَىٰ وَالْبَصِيرُ أَفَلَا تَتَفَكَّرُونَ

"Is the blind equivalent to the seeing? Then will you not think?"

Verily, Quran is the truth from God and is our guidance throughout life. It is our cure and inner serenity.

Allah says in Surah Al-Isra, verse 82 :

وَنُنَزِّلُ مِنَ الْقُرْآنِ مَا هُوَ شِفَاءٌ وَرَحْمَةٌ لِّلْمُؤْمِنِينَ ۙ وَلَا يَزِيدُ الظَّالِمِينَ إِلَّا خَسَارًا 82)

"And We send down of the Qur'an that which is healing and mercy for the believers, but it does not increase the wrongdoers except in loss."

Allah says in Surah Yunus, verse 57 :

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَتْكُم مَّوْعِظَةٌ مِّن رَّبِّكُمْ وَشِفَاءٌ لِّمَا فِي الصُّدُورِ وَهُدًى وَرَحْمَةٌ لِّلْمُؤْمِنِينَ (57)

"O people, there has to come to you instruction from your Lord and healing for what is in the hearts and guidance and mercy for the believers."

Allah says in Surah Fussilat , verse 44 :

قُلْ هُوَ لِلَّذِينَ آمَنُوا هُدًى وَشِفَاءٌ ۖ وَالَّذِينَ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ فِي آذَانِهِمْ وَقْرٌ وَهُوَ عَلَيْهِمْ عَمًى ۚ أُولَٰئِكَ يُنَادَوْنَ مِن مَّكَانٍ بَعِيدٍ (44)

" Say ( O prophet of Allah ) "It is, for those who believe, a guidance and cure." And those who do not believe - in their ears is deafness, and it is upon them blindness. Those are being called from a distant place."

The Quran is a guidance from the darkness of ignorance to the light of Allah's infinite mercy. The Quran is a cure from ignorance.

Those who are mentioned in verse 44 from Surah Fussilat, those who are too arrogant to see the truth are like someone who is so far away, and when that person is being called from a far distance, that person can neither hear nor see clearly the source of the voice that is calling him or her. So, those who are disbelievers are like this person. They are so far away from the truth, because their hearts have been covered by ignorance and disgrace. Allah says in Surah As-saffat , verse 35 :

إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا إِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ (35)

"Indeed they, when it was said to them, "There is no deity but Allah," were arrogant"

Allah has blessed us with so many gifts, but we seldom thank him for that and we seldom see and appreciate all the blessings.

Allah orders us to think, to observe, to see, to read, to seek knowledge, to get to know him so that we get to know ourselves as well.

There is a good saying : " science is a profound source of spirituality. "

The development of science must get you closer to Allah. Blind rationality ruins you and makes you less than a human and worse than an animal. Religion and specifically the absolute truth : Islam, and science must get you closer to Allah.

To be a Muslim is to be a thinker, a deep thinker and a deep observer and a reader and a seeker of good knowledge.

Dear Allah, I love you ❤

Dear Islam, you are home
